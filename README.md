## 📖 How to Use

### Dashboard (Your Daily View)
1. **Financial Health Score** - Comprehensive rating 0-100
2. **Achievements** - Track your wins and milestones
3. **Streak Counter** - Months you've been on track
4. **Debt Distribution Chart** - Visual breakdown by debt
5. **Pro Tips** - Daily motivation and financial advice

### Payoff Strategy (Choose Your Method)
```
Snowball Method:
1. List debts smallest to largest
2. Pay minimums on everything
3. Put extra money toward smallest debt
4. Once paid off, move to next
→ Best for: Motivation and momentum

Avalanche Method:
1. List debts highest to lowest interest rate
2. Pay minimums on everything
3. Put extra money toward highest interest
4. Once paid off, move to next
→ Best for: Saving the most money mathematically

Custom Order:
1. Arrange debts in YOUR order
2. Focus on priorities that matter to you
→ Best for: Personal preference and flexibility
```

### Making Extra Payments (Debt Snowflakes)
1. Go to Transactions
2. Click "Add Transaction"
3. Select "Debt Snowflake"
4. Choose the debt
5. Enter amount (bonus, tax refund, etc)
6. Watch your payoff date decrease! 🎉

### Tracking Progress
1. **Dashboard** - See overall financial health
2. **Analysis** - Detailed metrics and trends
3. **Debts** - Individual debt progress bars
4. **Assets** - Net worth chart and breakdown
5. **Export** - Download CSV for analysis

## 💾 Data & Privacy

### Where Is My Data Stored?
All your data is stored **locally on your device** in browser storage. Nothing is sent to servers.

✅ **Yes.** Your data never leaves your device
✅ No external servers

✅ No advertisements

### How Do I Back Up My Data?
1. Go to Analysis page
2. Click "Export to CSV"

Your FinWise data will be deleted. Always export CSV regularly to have backups!

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Is FinWise really free?**
A: Yes! 100% free, no subscriptions, no hidden fees, forever.

**Q: Do I need to connect my bank account?**
A: No! FinWise doesn't link to any banks. You enter everything manually for complete privacy.

**Q: Can I use FinWise offline?**
A: Yes! Once loaded, FinWise works without internet. Changes sync when online.

**Q: How do I export my data?**
A: Go to Analysis → Export to CSV. Download anytime.

**Q: Can I share my data?**
A: Yes! Export CSV and share with accountant, financial advisor, or family.

**Q: Will my data sync across devices?**
A: Not automatically (by design for privacy). You can export from one device and manually import to another.

**Q: What's a Debt Snowflake?**
A: A one-time extra payment from a bonus, tax refund, or unexpected money. Helps payoff faster!

**Q: Which strategy is best?**
A: Depends on your goals:
- **Snowball** = Motivation & momentum
- **Avalanche** = Save most money
- **Custom** = Your priorities

**Q: How often should I update?**
A: Ideally monthly, but even quarterly helps. Consistency matters more than frequency!

**Q: Can I delete a debt?**
A: Yes, use the delete button on each debt card. Paid off? Delete it and celebrate!

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: No, but FinWise works great on mobile browsers. Add to home screen for app-like experience!

**Q: How do I change my payoff strategy?**
A: Go to Payoff Strategy page and select Snowball, Avalanche, or Custom.
